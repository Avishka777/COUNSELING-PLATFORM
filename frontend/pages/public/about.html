About ThinkCeylon

Breaking barriers in mental health support

Our Story

ThinkCeylon was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make mental health support accessible to everyone, regardless of their circumstances or comfort level with traditional therapy settings.

We recognized that many people avoid seeking help due to stigma, privacy concerns, or fear of judgment. ThinkCeylon eliminates these barriers by providing completely anonymous access to professional counseling and a supportive community.

What started as a small initiative has grown into a thriving platform helping thousands of users across the country take control of their mental wellbeing.
