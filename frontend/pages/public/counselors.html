About Dr. Johnson

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a licensed clinical psychologist with over 15 years of experience helping individuals overcome anxiety, depression, and life transitions. She takes a compassionate, evidence-based approach to therapy, combining cognitive-behavioral techniques with mindfulness practices.

Dr. Johnson received her PhD from Boston University and completed her clinical training at Massachusetts General Hospital. She's passionate about making mental health support accessible to everyone and has specialized training in online therapy modalities.

Therapeutic Approach

Dr. Johnson's approach is collaborative and tailored to each individual's needs. She primarily uses:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Identifying and changing negative thought patterns
  • Mindfulness-Based Therapy: Developing present-moment awareness
  • Solution-Focused Therapy: Building on existing strengths
  • Positive Psychology: Cultivating wellbeing and resilience
